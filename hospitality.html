Transportation Details

From Vijayawada Bus Stand to K L University

K L University is prominent as KLCE (Koneru Lakshmaiah College of Engineering), to all the public and private transportation sectors, available round the clock from bus stand. One can hire an Auto-rickshaw from Pandit Nehru Bus stand at the cost of Rs.150/-.

On secondary option one can board a bus (47V or 151V the most prevalent) till Kunchanapalli Bridge and then take a service auto to reach out for the campus. The whole expense will be around Rs.30. Service autos are available near low pass of the bridge.

From Vijayawada Railway Station to K L University

K L University is prominent as KLCE (Koneru Lakshmaiah College of Engineering), to all the public and private transportation sectors, available round the clock from bus stand. One can hire an Service auto from Vijayawada Railway Station at the cost of Rs.40/-

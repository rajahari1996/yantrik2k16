Registration Guidelines

We heartily welcome you to YANTRIK 2016. Here we have mentioned some points that are to be taken care of during the registration process.


1. Go through the registration link. By clicking on the Registration Link, you will be redirected into Eventbrite page.

3. On confirming enter all your information and submit

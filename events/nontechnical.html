Speck Events

TRUST WALK

In this event there will be two participants as partners. One of the two participants is blind folded. A destination is set to the blind folded participant. He/she should reach their destination overcoming the obstacles by hearing the directions given by his/her partner

TECHNCIAL STONES

In this event, alphabets are engraved on stones and scattered on ground which when combined in correct order form a word. The participants are given a clue about the word. He/she have to figure out the word and arrange the stones one over the other and form a stack to form the given word. While arranging the stones, soft balls are thrown at the person arranging stones. If the ball hits the participant, he/she loose points.

CAROM

In this event, a carom board is set up with obstacles on it. The participant should pot the coin into the pocket of carom board with striker without touching the obstacles

TRIKCY FUN

This is a game which tests your IQ and your capacity to think logically. Tricky fun is where the participants need to answer questions based on their logical thinking capacity and your ability to solve a tricky question.

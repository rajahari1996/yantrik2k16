CAD WAR

Modelling a component is one of the essential step in any Design process which can be efficiently achieved with the help of computer often known as computer aided design This makes modelling simpler and makes products much quality full. Yantrik '16 paves a platform for the participants to involve in this event and help themselves to realize their inherent talents of drawing and modelling which may indirectly help the to pave a path for bright career

Round 2:
In this round, 3-D figures will be given to the participants and they have to finish the drawing within the prescribed time limit on the provided drawing sheets.

ROBOT DESIGN COMPETITION

Automation has been the most trending and burning topic in day to day life. Automation enhance efficiency and quality of work which also replaces the human effort. The neccesisity of this automation is required in every field which is why this event involves making of different robots compatible for its basic purpose.


Rules and Regulations

1. The bot must be under any of the below mentioned fields.
a.Service robot
b.Agriculture robot
c.Nursing robot
d.Underwater bot
e.Unmanned ground robot
f. Unmanned air robot
g.Surgical & Diagnostic robot
h.Domestic assistant robot

2. Multidisciplinary robots are encouraged.


3. Round 1:
In this round participant has to give a presentation regading his robot. Presentation must cover all the points such as manufacturing techniques, software assistancce, visual networking, modelling. The same must be submitted in form of a report, both soft and hard copy, at the time of presentation. Report must be maximum of (2 pages).

4. Round 2:
In this round participant has to show the performance and functioning of his/her robot by a test run. The basic purpose of the bot must be served efficiently


5. Final result will be based on the report submitted and performance of first two rounds


6. Cash prize is higher than expected.

PRODUCT DESIGNS

Product design as a verb is the process of creating a new product to be sold by a business to its customers. A very broad concept, it is essentially the efficient and effective generation and development of ideas through a process that leads to new products. There are various product design processes and many focus on different aspects.


Rules and Regulations


1. The participant has to accomplish the given task with the given material.

2. Participant should complete this task in given time.

3. Evolution is done based on the finishing of the material and time taken to complete the task.

4. The task should be done using the given material and no extra material shall be provided.

5. Participants are responsible for the given tools and may ask for extra tools( If reasonable there may be a chance of providing extra tool).
